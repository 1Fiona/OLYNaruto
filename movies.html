MOVIES

Naruto the Movie:
Ninja Clash in the Land of Snow (2004)

Naruto Uzumaki, Sasuke Uchiha and Sakura Haruno watch a film starring Yukie Fujikaze, in a local cinema. Kakashi Hatake has summoned them to watch it as preparation for their next mission: to prevent Yukie, who plays Princess Gale in the films, from being captured during production of her latest film.

Naruto the Movie 2:
Legend of the Stone of Gelel (2005)

The events of this movie took place between the Mizuki Tracking and Bikōchū Search Missions. Naruto, Shikamaru and Sakura are on a mission to deliver a lost pet to a village when a mysterious knight appear to confront them.

Naruto the Movie 3:
Guardians of the Crescent Moon Kingdom (2006)

Exuberant ninja Naruto teams up with his pals Sakura and Kakashi to escort Prince Michiru and his son, Hikaru, to the Crescent Moon kingdom.

Naruto Shippuden the Movie
(2007)

Naruto must protect the priestess Shion and, in doing so, help her fulfil her mission to defeat an evil demon and save the world, while also trying to escape the prediction of his own death.

Naruto Shippuden the Movie:
Bonds (2008)

A mysterious group of ninja called the Sora-nin from the Sky Country makes a surprise attack on Konoha. This is because Konoha nearly destroyed the Sky Country during the last Shinobi World War, yet they survived and now they're after Konoha and the Fire Country for revenge.

Naruto Shippuden the Movie:
The Will of Fire (2009)

Four ninjas who guard the Hidden Villages of Cloud Stone Mist and Sand disappear from their villages and another ninja war will break out if they are not found.

Naruto Shippuden the Movie:
The Lost Tower (2010)

Caught up in a chakra, Naruto is sent back in time to the city of Loran. Naruto might be able to return to his own time, and a chance encounter in the past might save the future.

Naruto Shippuden the Movie:
Blood Prison (2011)

Naruto is convicted of a crime he did not commit and sent to an inescapable prison. Although he plans to break out, he must find trustworthy allies before attempting to escape.

Naruto Shippuden the Movie:
Road to Ninja (2012)

Tobi uses Limited Tsukuyomi to transport Naruto and Sakura into an alternate reality filled with polar opposites of their friends.

Naruto Shippuden the Movie:
The Last (2014)

Two years after the Fourth Shinobi World War, Naruto must stop Toneri Otsutsuki, a descendant of Hamura Otsutsuki, after Toneri causes the moon to descend toward Earth.

Boruto:
Naruto the Movie (2015)

Several years after the end of the Shinobi War, Naruto's son Boruto is about to enter the Chûnin exams alongside Sarada Uchiha and the mysterious Mitsuki.
